Professional and academic profile.

An Associate Professor of Strategic Management, Innovation and Entrepreneurship at An-Najah National University, with more than 20 years of experience in entrepreneurship development, digital transformation, institutional capacity building, startup support, and donor-funded project implementation.

Currently serving as Director of An-Najah Business Hub, with a focus on strengthening the university’s entrepreneurship ecosystem through incubation, mentoring, venture development, university–industry engagement, and student-led innovation initiatives.

The profile combines academic expertise in strategy, entrepreneurship, and innovation with practical experience in designing capacity-building programmes, supporting SMEs and startups, developing business development services, and adapting international good practices to the Palestinian context.
